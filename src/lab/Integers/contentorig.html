Representation of Integers and their Arithmetic

Introduction

Integers are primitive data structures provided by default by almost all processors and programming languages. In this lab we study the represenation of integers and the arithmetic operations on them.

Objective

The objective of this experiment is to learn about representation of signed and unsigned integers. Specifically you will learn the advantages of using 2's complement representation of numbers for performing arithmetic over other representations..

Assignments

Check the isomorphic structure between unsigned representation of numbers and 2's complement representation of numbers with respect to binary addition.
